How to get your Brawlhalla ID
Go into your Brawlhalla Inventory and look for "User ID: 123456" in the top right

• /claim create create: NUMBER

Claim / Link a Brawlhalla Account to your Discord Account via Brawlhalla ID

• /claim update update: NUMBER

Update your currently linked Brawlhalla ID to another ID

• /claim delete

Remove your currently linked Brawlhalla ID from your Discord Account
